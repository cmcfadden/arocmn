About the club

AROC-MN is a group of Alfa Romeo enthusiasts in Minnesota. We get together for meetups over coffee, group drives, and social activities. Whether you're driving a brand new Stelvio, an 8C 2900, or you just love the brand, we encourage you to get in touch or join. We're always happy to chat or answer any quesitons. You can reach us at contact@alfamn.org
